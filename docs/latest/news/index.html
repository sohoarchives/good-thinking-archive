An important message from Good Thinking

To everyone who uses Good Thinking, we regret to inform you that this service will be closing on 31 January 2026. This has been a difficult decision after eight years of supporting Londoners. If you’re struggling with your mental health, you can access our website until January or get help from the NHS and other support organisations. For more information please see our FAQs page.

#ZeroSuicideLDN campaign hits 400,000 mark ahead of World Suicide Prevention Day

News|

LAST REVIEWED 9 September 2024

Suicide is the leading cause of death in people aged 15–29, and the biggest killer of men under 49 in the UK. In London, the sad truth is suicide is the cause of death of around 10 Londoners every week. Launched by the Mayor of London in September 2019 and led by ThriveLDN, the citywide #ZeroSuicideLDN campaign has now supported more than 400,000 people across the capital to access free, online suicide prevention training – equipping them with the skills they may need to help someone in crisis.
