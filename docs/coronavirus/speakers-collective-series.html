To everyone who uses Good Thinking, we regret to inform you that this service will be closing on 31 January 2026. This has been a difficult decision after eight years of supporting Londoners. The Speakers Collective Mini-Series

Good Thinking worked with The Speakers Collective to produce a mini-series that explores how COVID-19 has affected people with lived experience of mental health.

Living through the COVID-19 pandemic has been challenging for us all. Whether you spent more time at home, went to work as an essential worker or home schooled your children, COVID-19 led to a huge change in the way we were able to lead our lives. After so many months of restrictions, you might have been struggling with boredom or loneliness or be stressed about your job and finances. It’s important to remember that you’re not alone.

Good Thinking worked with The Speakers Collective to produce a mini-series that explores how COVID-19 has affected people with lived experience of mental health. The videos share a range of lived experience and provide advice and suggestions that might help you or people you know. Please feel free to share the video links.
